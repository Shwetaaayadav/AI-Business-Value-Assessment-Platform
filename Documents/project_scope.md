# Project Scope

## Project Overview

The AI Business Value Assessment Platform is designed to identify, evaluate, and prioritize AI investment opportunities using customer support, customer behavior, and business performance data.

The platform combines analytics, machine learning, business intelligence, and decision-support techniques to help organizations determine where AI can generate the greatest value.

---

# In Scope

## Customer Support Analytics

Analysis of customer support operations to understand:

* Ticket distribution
* Queue workload
* Priority patterns
* Language distribution

---

## Customer Voice Intelligence

Analysis of customer communications to identify:

* Complaint categories
* Customer pain points
* Emerging topics
* Customer sentiment

---

## Retail Analytics

Analysis of transactional retail data to understand:

* Revenue performance
* Product performance
* Customer purchasing behavior
* Geographic performance

---

## Customer Segmentation

Identification of customer groups using:

* RFM Analysis
* Customer Lifetime Value
* Customer Personas

---

## AI Business Value Assessment

Evaluation of AI opportunities through:

* Opportunity Identification
* Automation Potential Assessment
* Initiative Scoring

---

## Decision Support

Prioritization of AI initiatives through:

* MCDA Scoring
* Initiative Ranking
* Recommendation Engine

---

## Visualization and Reporting

Development of:

* Executive Dashboard
* Customer Voice Dashboard
* Revenue Dashboard
* AI Opportunity Dashboard

---

## Application Layer

Development of a Streamlit application that enables users to:

* Upload business data
* Evaluate AI opportunities
* Generate recommendations
* Compare initiatives

---

# Out of Scope

The project does not include:

* Real-time production deployment
* Integration with enterprise systems
* Live chatbot implementation
* AI model training for production environments
* Automated decision execution
