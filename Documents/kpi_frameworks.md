# KPI Framework

## Overview

The KPI Framework defines the metrics used to evaluate customer operations, customer experience, business performance, and AI investment opportunities.

---

# Customer Support KPIs

## Ticket Volume

Purpose:

Measure overall support demand.

Formula:

Total Number of Tickets

---

## Queue Workload

Purpose:

Measure workload distribution across support functions.

Formula:

Tickets per Queue

---

## High Priority Ticket Rate

Purpose:

Measure operational risk.

Formula:

High Priority Tickets / Total Tickets

---

## Complaint Category Frequency

Purpose:

Identify recurring customer issues.

Formula:

Tickets per Complaint Category

---

# Customer Voice KPIs

## Complaint Frequency

Purpose:

Identify major customer pain points.

---

## Topic Frequency

Purpose:

Measure prevalence of discovered topics.

---

## Sentiment Score

Purpose:

Measure customer sentiment.

Range:

-1 to +1

---

## Negative Sentiment Rate

Purpose:

Measure dissatisfaction levels.

Formula:

Negative Tickets / Total Tickets
