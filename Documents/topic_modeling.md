# Topic Modeling

## Objective

Identify hidden themes and recurring issues within customer support tickets using Natural Language Processing techniques.

Unlike Complaint Categorization, which relies on predefined labels, Topic Modeling discovers themes directly from customer communications.

---

# Dataset

Customer Support Dataset

Columns Used:

* subject
* body

---

# Technique

BERTopic

Components:

* Sentence Transformers
* UMAP
* HDBSCAN

---

# Business Questions

1. What topics are customers discussing most frequently?

2. Are there emerging issues not captured by predefined tags?

3. Which topics represent operational risk?

4. Which topics represent opportunities for automation?

---

# Expected Outputs

* Topic Clusters
* Topic Frequencies
* Topic Keywords
* Topic-Based Business Insights

---

# Business Value

Topic Modeling enables organizations to:

* Identify emerging customer concerns
* Detect recurring operational issues
* Discover automation opportunities
* Support AI investment decisions
