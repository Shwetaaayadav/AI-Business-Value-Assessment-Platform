# Problem Statement

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become a major investment area for organizations across industries. Companies are increasingly implementing AI-powered solutions such as customer support chatbots, recommendation engines, virtual assistants, workflow automation tools, predictive analytics systems, and Generative AI assistants to improve business performance and customer experience.

Despite substantial investments in AI technologies, many organizations struggle to determine whether these initiatives generate measurable business value. Existing evaluation methods often focus on technical metrics such as model accuracy, response time, or system usage, while neglecting broader business outcomes such as customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, employee productivity, revenue growth, and return on investment.

Additionally, organizations frequently deploy multiple AI initiatives across different departments without a standardized framework to compare their effectiveness. This makes it difficult for decision-makers to identify which AI solutions should be expanded, optimized, or discontinued.

As a result, businesses face challenges in resource allocation, budget prioritization, and AI strategy planning.

Therefore, there is a need for a comprehensive AI Business Value Assessment Platform that evaluates AI initiatives using business-centric performance indicators and supports data-driven investment decisions.
