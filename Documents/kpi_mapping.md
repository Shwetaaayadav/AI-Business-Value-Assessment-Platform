# KPI to Column Mapping

## Customer Support Dataset

### Ticket Volume

Columns:
- subject
- type

Purpose:
Measure frequency of support requests.

---

### Priority Distribution

Columns:
- priority

Purpose:
Identify high-risk customer issues.

---

### Queue Analysis

Columns:
- queue

Purpose:
Analyze workload distribution.

---

### Issue Category Analysis

Columns:
- type
- tag_1 to tag_8

Purpose:
Identify common customer problems.

---

### Customer Sentiment Score

Columns:
- body

Technique:
Sentiment Analysis

Purpose:
Measure customer satisfaction indirectly.

---

### Support Quality Score

Columns:
- answer

Technique:
Text Analytics

Purpose:
Evaluate support response quality.

---

### Topic Discovery

Columns:
- body

Technique:
Topic Modeling

Purpose:
Discover recurring customer concerns.

---

## Online Retail Dataset

### Revenue

Columns:
- Quantity
- UnitPrice

Formula:

Revenue = Quantity × UnitPrice

---

### Customer Lifetime Value

Columns:
- CustomerID
- Quantity
- UnitPrice

Purpose:
Identify high-value customers.

---

### Purchase Frequency

Columns:
- CustomerID
- InvoiceNo

Purpose:
Measure customer engagement.

---

### Customer Recency

Columns:
- CustomerID
- InvoiceDate

Purpose:
Determine customer activity level.

---

### Product Performance

Columns:

Purpose:

### Geographic Revenue Analysis

Analyze regional performance.
