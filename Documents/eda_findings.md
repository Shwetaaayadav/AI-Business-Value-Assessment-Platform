# EDA Findings

## Customer Support Dataset

### Language Analysis

The dataset contains support tickets in two primary languages:

| Language | Ticket Count |
| -------- | ------------ |
| English  | 16,338       |
| German   | 12,249       |

### Business Insight

The organization appears to serve two major customer segments: English-speaking and German-speaking customers.

Potential business implications:

* AI support systems must support multilingual interactions.
* AI chatbot performance should be evaluated separately for English and German.
* Localization and translation capabilities may significantly impact customer experience.
