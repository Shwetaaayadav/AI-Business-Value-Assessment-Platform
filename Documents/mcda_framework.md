# Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA) Framework

## Overview

The platform uses a Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA) framework to evaluate AI initiatives across multiple dimensions.

Rather than relying on ROI alone, initiatives are assessed based on financial, customer, operational, workforce, and strategic performance.

---

# Evaluation Dimensions

## Financial Impact

Measures:

- ROI
- Cost Savings
- Revenue Impact
- Payback Period

Weight: 30%

---

## Customer Impact

Measures:

- Customer Satisfaction
- Resolution Rate
- Escalation Rate
- Retention Rate

Weight: 25%

---

## Operational Impact

Measures:

- Time Saved
- Automation Rate
- Error Reduction
- Process Efficiency

Weight: 20%

---

## Workforce Impact

Measures:

- Adoption Rate
- Productivity Improvement
- Employee Satisfaction

Weight: 10%

---

## Strategic Impact

Measures:

- Business Alignment
- Scalability
- Competitive Advantage
- Risk Assessment

Weight: 15%

---

# Final Score Calculation

Final Score =

(Financial × 0.30)

+

(Customer × 0.25)

+

(Operational × 0.20)

+

(Workforce × 0.10)

+

(Strategic × 0.15)

---

# Decision Categories

## Expand

Score: 80–100

Characteristics:

- High ROI
- Strong customer impact
- Strong adoption

Recommendation:

Expand investment and scale initiative.

---

## Optimize

Score: 60–79

Characteristics:

- Positive performance
- Improvement opportunities exist

Recommendation:

Optimize processes and improve adoption.

---

## Review

Score: 40–59

Characteristics:

- Moderate value
- Limited business impact

Recommendation:

Conduct detailed assessment.

---

## Terminate

Score: Below 40

Characteristics:

- Poor ROI
- Weak adoption
- Low strategic value

Recommendation:

Discontinue or replace initiative.
