# AI Business Value Assessment Platform

## Overview

Organizations are increasingly investing in AI-powered solutions such as chatbots, recommendation engines, workflow automation tools, and generative AI assistants. However, many companies struggle to determine whether these investments generate measurable business value.

This project aims to develop a Business Intelligence and Decision Support Platform that evaluates AI initiatives across financial, operational, customer, workforce, and strategic dimensions.

---

## Problem Statement

Organizations lack a standardized framework to evaluate and compare AI initiatives based on business outcomes.

---

## Objectives

- Measure ROI of AI initiatives
- Evaluate operational impact
- Assess customer-facing AI effectiveness
- Measure workforce adoption
- Generate investment recommendations
